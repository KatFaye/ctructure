Our Services

Ctructure is a database system that lawyers can use to retrieve legal information based on a set of attributes (e.g. publication year, content type or agency of legal document, etc). The database will only include Rwandan laws.

Why choosing us

As of now, Rwandan laws are only available as PDF documents and hard copies (unstructured data). As such, our project will be the first effort to turn Rwandan laws into structured data that can be queried using a set of attributes. We hope that this will reduce the amount of time lawyers spend on legal research. In addition, by showing additional information such as amended and repealed laws, we hope to increase the accuracy of legal information.

Advance Features

1. Showing laws that are no longer up-date specifically laws that have been repealed or amended. This feature will attempt to facilitate lawyers to know which laws have been invalidated 2. Advance keyword search
